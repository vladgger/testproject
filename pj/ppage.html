Philips Iron

Description

The Philips iron features OptimalTEMP technology, ensuring no burns on any ironable fabric. With powerful steam capabilities, it effectively removes even the most stubborn creases, making ironing a breeze.

Key Features

  • OptimalTEMP Technology: Guarantees no burns on fabrics.
  • Powerful Steam: Continuous steam for effective crease removal.
  • Automatic Steam Control: Adjusts steam output based on ironing speed.
  • Vertical Steaming: Ideal for refreshing garments and delicate fabrics.
  • Easy Maintenance: Equipped with Easy De-Calc Plus system for descaling.

Specifications

  • Model: PerfectCare 8000 Series
  • Power: 2400 Watts
  • Water Tank Capacity: 1.8 liters
  • Steam Output: Up to 150 g/min
  • Weight: 1.2 kg
